Mashed Potatoes Recipe

From AllRecipes

Home Original Recipe

Description

A recipe for mashed potatoes from AllRecipes just in time for Thanksgiving!

Ingredients

  • Russet potatoes, peeled, halved length-wise - 3, large
  • Whole milk - 1/2 cup
  • Butter - 1/4 cup
  • Salt and black pepper - to taste

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Place potatoes in a large pot submerged in cold salted water by about 1 inch and cover. Bring to boil. Reduce heat to medium-low, still covered, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
  3. Strain potatoes, then return to pot. Turn heat to high and allow potatoes to dry for about 30 seconds. Turn off heat.
  4. Mash potatoes with a potato masher twice around the pot, then mash milk and butter until smooth and fluffy.
  5. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Whisk until evenly distributed.
