Cranberry Sauce Recipe

From AllRecipes

Home Original Recipe

Description

A recipe for cranberry sauce from AllRecipes just in time for Thanksgiving!

Ingredients

  • Cranberries - 12 oz.
  • White sugar - 1 cup
  • Orange juice - 1 cup

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Dissolve sugar in orange juice in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  3. Stir in cranberries and cook until they begin to pop and sauce thickens. About 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Remove from heat and place sauce in a bowl. Allow to cool and thicken.
