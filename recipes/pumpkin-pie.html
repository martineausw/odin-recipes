Pumpkin Pie Recipe

From AllRecipes

Home Original Recipe

Description

A recipe for pumpkin pie from AllRecipes just in time for Thanksgiving!

Ingredients

  • Pumpkin puree - 15 oz.
  • Sweetened condensed milk - 14 oz.
  • Eggs - 2, large
  • Ground cinnamon - 1 tsp.
  • Ground ginger - 1/2 tsp.
  • Ground nutmeg - 1/2 tsp.
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp.
  • Unbaked pie crust - 9 in.

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients and preheat oven to 425 F.
  2. Whisk pumpkin puree, condensed milk, eggs, cinnamon, ginger, nutmeg, salt in a medium bowl until smooth.
  3. Pour into crust. Bake in oven for 15 minutes.
  4. Reduce temperature to 350 F and continue baking to 35 to 40 minutes.
